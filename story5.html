About

The Enigmas at Red Hook is an Residency Project at DE-CONSTRUKT, which is a full-service design studio, photography darkroom, event and project space located in Brooklyn.

Ruixuan Li is an independent curator, writer, art researcher who is currently based in Ithaca, NY and Shenzhen, Guangdong, China.
